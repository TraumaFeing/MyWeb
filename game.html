My Game: Project Nova

Project Nova is a first-person roguelike shooter with procedurally generated levels, dynamic enemy AI, and a real-time upgrade system. Players explore diverse environments such as urban ruins, deep forests, and desert wastelands as they uncover the mystery behind a collapsing world.

Features:

  • 🎮 Realistic graphics powered by Unreal Engine 5
  • 🔫 Randomized weapons and upgrades
  • 🌍 Procedurally generated maps
  • 💾 Save/load system and progression tracking
